Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

A simple landing page takes 1 week, a business website 2-3 weeks, and e-commerce sites 4-6 weeks. Timeline depends on complexity and your feedback speed.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites I build are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

Do you provide hosting?

I can help you choose and set up hosting, or work with your existing hosting provider. I'll recommend the best options for your needs.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes! I build sites with user-friendly CMS platforms, and I'll provide training on how to update content yourself.

What if I need changes after launch?

I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages. Minor tweaks are included in the first 30 days post-launch.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! For projects over $1,000, I offer payment plans with 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.
